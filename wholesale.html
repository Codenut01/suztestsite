Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

What is the minimum order quantity? +
Minimum order values start at R2,500 for our Starter tier. There are no unit minimum per SKU — you can mix and match across our full catalogue to reach the minimum.
How long does delivery take? +
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Growth and Enterprise partners receive 1–2 day express delivery. Same-day options are available in Gauteng and Cape Town for qualifying orders.
Do you offer credit accounts? +
Yes. 30-day credit accounts are available for partners who have completed 3 successful orders and passed our standard credit check. Talk to your account manager for details.
Can I order specific brands? +
Absolutely. You can order any specific brand or SKU from our catalogue. Your account manager will provide a full product list with current availability and lead times.
What areas do you deliver to? +
We deliver nationwide across all 9 provinces of South Africa. Remote or rural areas may attract a delivery surcharge — your account manager will confirm this upfront.
What happens if products are damaged? +
All deliveries are inspected before dispatch. If goods arrive damaged, contact us within 48 hours with photos and we will arrange a replacement or credit on your next order.
