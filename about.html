Our Journey

25 YEARS OF
FLAVOUR

2000
The Beginning
Founded as a small family operation, SUZ Foods started with a single product line and a big dream — to bring quality snacks to every corner of South Africa.
2006
First Major Contract
Secured our first major retail partnership, supplying premium crisps to a network of 50+ stores. This milestone validated our commitment to quality and reliability.
2010
Product Line Expansion
Grew from chips to a full snack portfolio including corn snacks, puffed ranges, and our now-iconic biltong and droëwors selection.
2015
National Distribution
Expanded operations to reach all 9 provinces of South Africa, building a logistics network that ensures freshness and reliability at every delivery.
2020
International Brands
Became an authorised distributor for several international snack brands entering the South African market, adding over 200 new product lines.
2026
Today & Beyond
500+ product lines, thousands of retail partners, and a passionate family still at the helm — driving the next chapter of SUZ's flavour-filled story.
